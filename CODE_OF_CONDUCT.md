# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Participation in this project should remain respectful, technical, and constructive. Harassment, insults, discrimination, and bad-faith disruption are not acceptable.

## Expected Behavior

- Focus criticism on ideas, code, methods, and evidence.
- Be respectful in issues, pull requests, and review comments.
- Assume good faith unless there is clear evidence otherwise.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks or abusive language.
- Harassment or discriminatory conduct.
- Spam, trolling, or intentionally derailing technical discussion.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content, close discussions, or block contributors whose behavior harms the project environment.
